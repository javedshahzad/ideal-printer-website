Reverse Cut Frosted Sticker

About

Reverse cut frosted stickers are made by cutting the back of the vinyl, so they look right when viewed from the front of the glass. This makes them great for putting logos, names, or designs on the inside of windows, where people can see them from the outside. They keep the glass looking clean while showing off brands in a simple and elegant way.


These stickers are perfect for store windows, office lobbies, or any glass area open to the public. Since they are placed on the inside, they are safe from bad weather and damage. Ideal Printers provides custom reverse cut options that help promote your brand while keeping the frosted vinyl's privacy and light-softening features.

Standard Cut Frosted Sticker

About

Standard cut frosted stickers have shapes, text, or logos cut out from the front side of the vinyl. These designs stick to the front of glass surfaces, making them great for branding office walls, signs, and indoor glass areas. The frosted look gives privacy, while the cut-outs let you see through where needed.


At Ideal Printers, our standard cut frosted vinyl is made to have sharp edges and a smooth matte finish. They are a popular choice for businesses that want to add a subtle design or branding to their glass spaces while still keeping a professional look.
Cutout Style
Exact Cut

The graphics and letters on your decal will be cut to exact shape with inner spaces weeded/picked out.

Gradient Frosted Sticker

About

Gradient frosted stickers have a soft shift from full frost to clear, creating a nice fade effect. They work well in modern offices where partial privacy is needed, like in meeting rooms, shared spaces, and reception areas, without blocking all light.


Ideal Printers carefully makes and puts on gradient frosted vinyl, keeping a good look while separating spaces. The gradient adds a stylish touch to glass walls without stopping light from coming through, keeping the office feeling open and balanced. They are a smart choice for offices that value openness and design.
