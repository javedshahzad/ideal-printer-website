Frequently Asked Questions

Regular stencil vinyl can only be used once, but our low tack stencil vinyl is made for multiple uses. You can stick it on clean, smooth surfaces 2 to 3 times.

Our adhesive stencil stickers work well on smooth surfaces like sanded wood, concrete, metal, glass, or painted walls. They can also stretch, making it easy to use them on curved shapes.

Standard adhesive stencil stickers are meant for one-time use, which helps create sharp edges and prevents smudging. If you need to paint several times, we can offer heavy-duty Mylar or plastic stencils.
