Cutout Style

Standard Cut

Each letter and object in the design is cut directly from the vinyl material and installed on the surface leaving no background.

Reverse Cut

Each design element is cut out of the vinyl making that portion hollow. Having removed the cut out elements, the vinyl is then applied on the surface with the background.
