    PVC foam board, also known as forex board, is a durable material for durable prints. In contrast to cardboard or paper they are not prone to twist or wrinkle easily, which means your prints remain in place throughout your event. Their lightweight construction allows them to be portable and simple to put up, while their sturdy surface ensures they stay in place when placed on easels, walls or even on stands. This long-lasting durability coupled with vivid printing makes foam boards an effective advertising tool for companies across Lahore and across Pakistan. Another advantage of PVC foam boards is its flexibility.
