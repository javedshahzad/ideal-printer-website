Essential Insights for Supporting the Cause

The power of knowledge empowers us to take action, which is why Ideal Printers is committed to sharing this knowledge. Here are some key elements to help us understand better and create changes:

  • Worldwide Prevalence: Breast cancer is the most frequent cancer in women around the world, with an incidence of 1 out of 8 women during their lives.
  • 2025 projections: 2025 Projections: In the US alone, 316,950 women will be diagnosed with an aggressive breast cancer this year, together with 59,080 cases involving the ductal carcinoma in situ (DCIS).
  • Mortality Effect: The world's Breast cancer will claim around 670,000 lives between 2022 and 2023. The incidence rate increased by 1 to 5 percent annually across many regions.
  • Early Detection Results: If it is detected early and in a local area, the 5-year survival rate can reach 99percent, which is a statistic Ideal Printers emphasizes for timely screenings.
  • Preventive measures: Mammograms that are routinely scheduled at 40 years old, regular self-exams, and a healthy diet that is balanced and includes exercise can greatly reduce the risk and improve the outcomes.

These details provided by Ideal Printers highlight why Pink October is crucial. It's an international campaign to inform, screen, and encourage and break the silence about the issue in communities like ours.
