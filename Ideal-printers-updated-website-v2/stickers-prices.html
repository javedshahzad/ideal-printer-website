Die Cut Stickers

Die cut stickers are stickers cut to the exact shape of your design. They are excellent for displaying your brand or message. In contrast to standard stickers available in simple shapes, die cut stickers enable you to be very creative, whether you're for a logo, icon, or any unique shape. Here at Ideal Printers, we utilize cutting-edge cutting tools to ensure the edges are cut cleanly and the lines are crisp, regardless of how intricate your design is.


Our stickers are perfect for giveaways, packaging, laptop covers, bottles, window promotion, and product labels. We have various finishes such as matte, gloss, and clear vinyl. Our materials are water-resistant and durable, thus are perfect for both indoor and outdoor purposes. Whether you're a new company looking to make an impression or an artist in need of that extra something, our die cut stickers are designed with you in mind. Frequently Asked Questions

We can accommodate same-day die-cut stickers printing, provided that your artwork is print-ready and submitted prior to our daily cut-off time. Please be advised that same-day services are fully dependent on the required quantity and type of sticker.

Our design team is there to offer professional help in ensuring that you achieve the perfect vector cut path and that there is sufficient bleed for a perfect finish.

The transparent die-cut stickers that we offer are completely water resistant since they're printed with high-quality vinyl that is not affected by moisture, oil, or the outdoors.They're the perfect choice for packaging and freezer items.

We can print multiple distinct designs in one sheet through the use of our digital printing technology and die-cutting technology. Through this method, you can combine different shapes, sizes, and designs in one page.

For optimal results, we suggest that you work with vector formats such as AI, EPS, or PDF files. This is due to the scalability of the images. When working with raster files such as PNG or JPEG files, ensure that they have a high resolution of at least 300 DPI and contain a border.
