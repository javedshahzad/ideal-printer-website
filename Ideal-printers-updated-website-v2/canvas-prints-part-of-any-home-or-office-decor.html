Here are some tips to help you keep your canvas prints looking their best:

  • Use a soft, dry microfiber cloth or feather duster.
  • Keep away from direct sunlight to prevent fading.
  • Maintain moderate humidity levels, avoid damp areas.
  • Use clean hands and support larger prints with both hands.
  • Protect from spills and hang in dry areas.
  • Securely mount with appropriate hanging hardware.
  • Check for indicators of damage regularly.
  • For deep cleaning or repair, get professional assistance.
