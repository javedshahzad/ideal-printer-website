Traffolyte / PVC / Acrylic Labels

About

Well liked among the people of Lahore are Traffolyte labels, which are known for their durability and fine engravability. They are made of stacked PVC sheets and have applications in wire tags, machine plates, and device markers. These are ideal for commercial and industrial applications due to their polished surface that does not degrade with time. Traffolyte labels can be customized based on your unique requirement with different patterns. Both in furniture and machinery, they provide for long-lasting and clear identification.
