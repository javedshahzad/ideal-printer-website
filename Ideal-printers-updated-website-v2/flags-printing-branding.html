Frequently Asked Questions

Flag printing is the process of creating custom flags with designs, logos, or messages using digital or traditional printing techniques. It involves transferring artwork onto fabric to produce high-quality, durable flags for various purposes like events, advertising, or decoration.

Printed Flags are usually either digitally printed or screen printed. The process involves creating a high-resolution digital image of the design, preparing the fabric, and then applying the image through specialized printers or screens.

We use materials such as Knitted Polyester, Satin Gloss, and Super Matt for printing flags.
