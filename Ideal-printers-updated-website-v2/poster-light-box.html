Frequently Asked Questions

The ideal choice for a light box is often acrylic since it's strong, not heavy, and lets light shine through smoothly, which helps images appear sharp and bright.

When picking a light box, think about its size, the material it's made of, such as clear acrylic, and LED lights for energy savings. Also, decide if you need it for inside or outside use, how much you want to spend.

An LED lightbox is a type of display that uses LED technology to light up pictures from behind a see-through panel. This gives a bright and energy-saving light that makes the images easier to see.

To create a lightweight poster display, you usually build a setup with a light source placed behind a see-through sheet. This lets the light shine through the sheet to brighten the poster from the back.
