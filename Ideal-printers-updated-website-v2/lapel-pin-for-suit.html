What is a Lapel Pin?

A lapel pin is a small ornament that is attached to a suit jacket lapel, and a lapel is defined as a flap of material on the front of a garment, especially a suit jacket, just below the collar.

It has emerged as one of the popular executive accessories currently available. Your brand, your personality, your milestone, or simply your finishing touch, a lapel pin for a suit jacket can do it all.

The Proper Position for Your Suit Lapel Pin

First and foremost, getting the position right is the most critical aspect of wearing a lapel pin for your suit correctly and stylishly. To do this, you need to ensure that your pin is always placed on your left lapel, specifically through the buttonhole, which is present in most suit lapels, referred to as the boutonniere hole. This is a small hole or loop created specifically to hold your lapel pin or flower.

If your suit is not provided with a buttonhole, you can place your pin about four inches down from your left shoulder seam, positioned centrally on your lapel.

Types of Lapel Pins & When to Wear Them in Lahore and Across Pakistan

Type of lapel pin for a suit that one needs is largely dependent on the occasion, personal style, and the intended message. Here are the popular types of lapel pins available in Lahore.

Spinner Lapel Pin

A spinner lapel pin is an accessory with a rotating piece that moves and spins freely as part of the pin's design. This creates a dynamic and eye-catching piece that is also interactive and unique. The spinner lapel pin is ideal for creative professionals and businesses that want an effective promotional tool for corporate events, and anyone who wants a lapel pin for suit that is sure to spark an interesting conversation and leave a lasting impression.

Chain Link Charm

A chain link charm lapel pin is a type of lapel pin that has a small charm hung by a chain. This type of lapel pin is considered elegant and refined and exudes luxury. Chain link charm lapel pins are best for formal business events and corporate functions such as award shows and business meetings.
These types of lapel pins are considered the epitome of elegance and sophistication and are considered the hallmark of a distinguished individual.

Glitter Fill Lapel Pin

A glitter fill lapel pin is a pin with a glittering, glitter filled design that looks absolutely stunning and is sure to add a touch of vibrant, glamorous, and visually appealing flair to any suit or outfit.
These pins are ideal for special celebration events, corporate events, launches, and any other occasion where you want your lapel pin for a suit to really shine with a hint of sparkle and excitement.

Country Flags Lapel Pin

Glow-in-the-Dark

How to Pair Your Lapel Pin with Any Event?

The right lapel pin for the suit must be paired with the right event in order to make the best possible impression. For formal corporate meetings, business presentations, and boardroom presentations.

For gala events, awards shows, celebrations, and formal events, the right lapel pin for the suit is one that is luxurious, sophisticated and exquisite, with glitter fill, a chain link charm, and a metallic finish in order to add a touch of executive elegance.

Style Tips for Wearing Lapel Pins

To follow simple but effective style tips in wearing your lapel pin for suit with maximum confidence and elegance, you should note the following: You should coordinate the color or finish of your lapel pin with that of your other accessories, such as your watch, cufflinks, and belt buckle.

Your lapel pin should be proportional in size to the size of your lapel, a smaller size is appropriate for a narrower lapel, while a larger size is appropriate for a wider lapel. You should also note to wear only one lapel pin at a time to maintain a clean and sophisticated executive image.

Common Mistakes to Avoid When You Wear Lapel Pins

The proper position for your pin should follow the boutonniere hole, which you need to use as a guide while you avoid placing it too high or too low on the lapel.

You should not wear a lapel pin that exceeds your lapel size because this will lead to fabric damage and create an unbalanced appearance.

The combination of multiple accessories with your lapel pin should be avoided because the use of a Pocket square together with a tie pin and lapel pin creates an excessive and disorganized appearance. You should never wear a novelty or casual pin to a formal business event because it will damage your professional reputation and executive presence.
