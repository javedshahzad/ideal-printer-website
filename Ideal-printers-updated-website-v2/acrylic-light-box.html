Frequently Asked Questions

An acrylic light box is a display made of acrylic material with a see-through front part that lights up from the inside using LEDs to show pictures or ads.

The price of acrylic signs can change, but they are usually seen as reasonably priced, offering a good mix of being strong and looking nice.

The ideal material for light boxes is usually acrylic, which is famous for its clear appearance, strength, and good light-transmitting properties.

An acrylic light sign board is a sign made of see-through acrylic that lights up from behind, usually with LED lights, making it bright and eye-catching.

Backlit acrylic is a type of acrylic that lets light pass through its surface, making it suitable for signs and displays that light up.
