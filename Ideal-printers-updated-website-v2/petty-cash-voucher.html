Petty Cash Vouchers Printing in Lahore

A petty cash voucher is usually a small form that is used to document a disbursement (payment) from a petty cash fund. Petty cash vouchers are also referred to as petty cash receipts. Petty cash funds are maintained to provide a company with fast access to small amounts of cash for minor, non-routine purchases. Petty cash vouchers are used to maintain financial control of petty cash accounts, preventing abuse or mismanagement of the funds.
