How to measure

  • Allow your arm to relax and measure (you may need assistance) around your bicep. choose a size that is just smaller than your measurement to ensure a snug fit. the elastic will expand with arm movement.
  • For example, if your arm circumference is 30cm, choose the Small size armband.
  • Remember to leave enough room for fitting over a shirt or heavy jacket.
