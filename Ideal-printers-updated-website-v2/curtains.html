Steps to measure:

Measure the Pole Width
  • Measure the pole, not the window
  • If installed, measure between the decorative ends (finials)
  • If not, plan for a pole 15 cm above the window and 15 cm past each side
Decide the Curtain Drop (Length)
  • Measure from the top of the pole to where you want the curtain to end (sill or floor)
Decide the Curtain Width (Fullness)
  • For a neat, full look, use curtain fabric = 2x pole width
  • Example: 100 cm pole ? 200 cm total curtain width
  • For customized size, tell us your preferred width per panel
Share Your Details
  • Provide pole length, drop, and desired fullness (e.g., 2x)
  • If you don't have a pole yet, share your window width - we'll calculate the right size for you
