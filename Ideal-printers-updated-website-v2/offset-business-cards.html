Benefits of Offset printing Business cards
  • High image quality.
  • Works on a wide range of printing Stocks and Paper Material including Textured and Embossed Papers. The thickness of the stock can go up to 350gsm.
  • Offset printing has a front-end cost load. Short runs may have a high unit cost. But as quantities increase, the unit cost goes down with offset printing. Very short runs can be much more cost effective with digital printing; while larger quantities are likely to have a lower unit cost with offset printing.
  • If you're planning to print using the Pantone" Matching System, offset printing will give you the best match, since it uses actual Pantone" ink. Digital printing simulates the color using a four-color matching process, so digital printers may offer less accurate color matching on projects.

Terms and Conditions:
  • Print colour may vary from print run to print run and OR from job to job OR from front to back.
  • A reasonable variation in colour between original sample / proofs and the completed job, will be considered acceptable print quality.
  • There may be some colour variation between what the client sees on screen and what the final product looks like. There will be no reprints at the supplier's expense due to colour variations.
  • Acceptable variations may be up to 10% of the selected pantone or CMYK colour values.
Important Note:

Fluorescent Pantone colors and metallic colors other than Gold Pantone 872 and Silver Pantone 877 require additional charges. Contact us for a quote.
