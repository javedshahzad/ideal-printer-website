Non Woven Bags

About

Our non-woven bags are inexpensive, sturdy, and lightweight. These non-woven bags are produced from spun-bonded polypropylene and have a hatched woven pattern pressed into them to give them the appearance of being woven. These bags can be printed with your brand and design colour.


Why non woven bags?
  • Customers can reuse non-woven bags, promoting ones company
  • Extremely strong and lightweight
  • Available in a variety of colours

Uses

  • Food and drink outlets
  • Exhibitions and events
